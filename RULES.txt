1. Press PLUS sign button to choose how many colors in a sequence you want to guess,
2. Press PLAY button to start the game,
3. You can repeat a sequence 3 times(the small number next to the play sign) for a game only during your turn by pressing the play button,
4. The number at the top is your highest score,
5. The number at the center is your current sequence,
6. Press RESET button anytime you want to stop the game and zero the counters.
